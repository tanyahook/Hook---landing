Tahira's read on your customer

The hook

"I stopped buying expensive serums when I found out what was actually in them."

Curiosity plus mild controversy. Speaks directly to the cynical, ingredient-conscious buyer at cold awareness. She feels seen, not sold to. That's the entire job of a cold ad.

The brief
Funnel stage
Cold awareness. Don't pitch. Not yet.
Format
UGC-style video, 15–30 sec, portrait. Real, not produced.
What to film
Someone checking the back of a bottle, visibly surprised. Text overlay only. No voiceover.
Audience
Broad + interest stack: clean beauty, skincare — F 25–45
Test against
"My skin after 3 weeks of actually reading labels."
